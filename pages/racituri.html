Răcituri

About

Roughly translated to “cold things”, you might think: damn that looks gross; and you would be right. It truly does look disgusting. However, the taste blin. Give it a chance and you won’t be disappointed. Maybe don’t look at it too much.

Ingredients

  • 1 pork knuckle (about 1 kg)
  • 2 pig feet (about 350 grams each)
  • 2 garlic heads
  • a lot of pepper
  • a lot of salt

How to

First, cut the knuckle in half to ensure thorough cooking. Put the meats in a pressure cooker and cover them with water. Add a tablespoon of salt. Cook for about an hour. Put the cooked meats on a large plate and allow them to cool completely.

DO NOT THROW THE BROTH. THAT’S THE JELLY.

Crush the garlic and put it in the broth together with pepper to taste. Let it sit while the meats cool.

When cooled, separate the meat from the bones. You can have the pieces of meat as large or small as you like. Put the pieces of meat in a large bowl and cover them with the broth. Put the bowl in the fridge and let it sit overnight.

Next day, glorious răcituri are ready to be consumed with bread, potato or as is.
